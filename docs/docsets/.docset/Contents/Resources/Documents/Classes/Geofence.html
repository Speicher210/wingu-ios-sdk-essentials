Geofence

public final class Geofence : Channel

Representation of geofence area from wingu portal, Configurable in wingu portal contains usually already prepared content, without extra fetches (like in a case of iBeacon).
In case of missing content in geofence by default it will not be shown in WinguLocations and will not be returned through delegate methods.

  • boundaries

    boundaries of the geofence. In case of shape different than circle it will be redrawn to make circle with the same surface area and central point.
